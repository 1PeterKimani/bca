Kenyans Caught Sneaking 1 Billion Contraband From Dubai

Two Kenyans are the individuals who were arrested at an Indian Airport...

Posted by newstoday.co.ke on August 24, 2022

World media outlets reported on Sunday, April 3, that the trio landed at the airport in Kolkata before they were immediately arrested by the Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI).The three, who included two Kenyans and a Malawian national, were found with drugs of the type heroin valued at Ksh1.7 billion shillings (Rs 113 Crore). During recent months, India has emerged among the top destination for drugs and goods ranging from drugs to fake Gold bars.After arrest the two Kenyans were said to argue that they were in the country on a medical visa, specifically for a check-up in New Delhi. In the last two months alone, more than five hundred Africans have been arrested upon touchdown in the South Asian country for being in possession illegal goods. At the beginning of March, two other Kenyans were arrested by Indian security agencies after they were nabbed trying to sneak heroin valued at Ksh900 million (60 million Rupees) in one of the country's airports.
